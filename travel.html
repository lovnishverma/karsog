Frequent Questions

Everything you need to know before you go.

Best time to visit Karsog?
April to June is perfect for pleasant weather. January to February is ideal if you want to witness snowfall in the upper reaches like Shikari Devi.
How is the mobile network?
Jio and Airtel have strong 5G coverage in Karsog market and Chindi. Connectivity might be intermittent during the Shikari Devi trek.
Is it safe for solo female travelers?
Absolutely. Himachal Pradesh is one of the safest states. Our verified homestays and drivers ensure a completely secure environment for solo travelers.
What is the cancellation policy?
We offer a flexible policy. Full refund if cancelled 7 days prior to the trip. 50% refund if cancelled 3 days prior.
