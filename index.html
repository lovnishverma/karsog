About Karsog Valley Tourism

Located at 31.38°N, 77.20°E in Mandi District, Himachal Pradesh, India. Karsog Valley is a pristine Himalayan destination renowned for 50+ ancient temples, Shikari Devi Peak (3,359m), apple orchards, trekking routes, and authentic Pahari mountain culture. Elevation: 1,800-3,359m | Population: 93,126 | Area: 524 km²
