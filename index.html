3-Day Itinerary

The perfect plan to see it all

DAY 1

Arrival & Temple Run

Arrive via Shimla. Check into Manat Guest House. Visit the ancient Mamleshwar Mahadev (5000-year-old grain) & Kamaksha Devi.

DAY 2

Shikari Devi Trek

Early morning drive to the base. Trek to the roofless temple (3359m). Enjoy 360° panoramic views. Picnic at apple orchards on return.

DAY 3

Chindi & Departure

Visit the scenic Chindi hamlet. Buy local Rajma and hand-knitted socks at the market. Depart for Delhi/Chandigarh.

Travel FAQ

How are the road conditions?
The road from Shimla via Tattapani is decent but curvy. The route from Mandi via Rohanda is scenic but can be narrow in places. SUVs are recommended.
Is mobile network available?
Jio and Airtel have strong 4G coverage in the main town and most tourist spots. Connectivity might drop near Shikari Devi top.
Best time for snow?
January and February are the best months to witness snowfall, especially in upper reaches like Shikari Devi and Chindi.
