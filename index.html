3-Day Escape

Maximize your weekend with this optimized plan.

Day 1

Arrival & Temple Run

Arrive via Shimla (108km) or Mandi. Check-in at Chindi. Visit the ancient Mamleshwar Mahadev (see the 5000-year-old grain) & Kamaksha Devi temple.

Day 2

The Shikari Devi Trek

Early morning drive to the base. Trek to the roofless temple (3359m). Witness 360° Himalayan views. Picnic at apple orchards on the way back.

Day 3

Local Culture & Departure

Visit the local market. Buy organic Rajma and hand-knitted socks. Enjoy a final Pahari meal (Siddu with Ghee) before departing.

Frequent Questions

How are the road conditions?
The road from Shimla via Tattapani is decent but curvy. The route from Mandi via Rohanda is scenic but can be narrow. SUVs are recommended, though hatchbacks can manage with care.
Is mobile network available?
Yes, Jio and Airtel have strong 4G coverage in Karsog town and Chindi. Connectivity might be intermittent near the Shikari Devi top.
Best time for snow?
January and February are the best months to witness snowfall, especially in the upper reaches like Shikari Devi, Kamrunag, and Chindi.
