Perfect 3-Day Itinerary

How to spend your time in the valley

Day 1

Arrival & Temple Run

Arrive via Shimla or Mandi. Check into your homestay. Visit the ancient Mamleshwar Mahadev temple (see the 5000-year-old grain) and Kamaksha Devi temple in the evening.

Day 2

The Peak Adventure

Early morning drive and trek to Shikari Devi Temple (Roofless temple). Enjoy 360¬∞ views of the Himalayas. On the way back, stop at apple orchards for fresh juice.

Day 3

Relaxation & Chindi

Visit the picturesque hamlet of Chindi. Have a picnic at the PWD rest house lawns. Shop for local Rajma and woolen socks before heading back.

Frequently Asked Questions

How are the road conditions?
The road from Shimla via Tattapani is decent but curvy. The route from Mandi via Rohanda is scenic but can be narrow in places. SUVs are recommended but hatchbacks can manage.
Is mobile network available?
Yes, Jio and Airtel have strong 4G coverage in the main town and most tourist spots. Connectivity might drop near Shikari Devi top.
Best time to visit for snow?
January and February are the best months if you want to witness snowfall, especially in the upper reaches like Shikari Devi and Chindi.
